# MARS 2044: Ethical Guidelines

## Purpose

These guidelines are established to ensure the responsible and ethical use of the Mars 2044 simulation.

## Principles

* **Respect for Human Dignity:** All interactions within the simulation must respect human dignity and avoid harmful or discriminatory content.
* **Data Privacy:** User data must be protected and used only for authorized purposes.
* **Transparency:** The simulation's purpose and limitations must be clearly communicated to users.
* **Beneficence:** The simulation should be used for the benefit of humanity and to promote positive values.
* **Non-Maleficence:** The simulation should not be used to cause harm or suffering.

## Oversight

* An ethical review board will oversee the development and operation of the simulation.
* Regular ethical assessments will be conducted to ensure compliance with these guidelines.

## User Responsibility

* Users are responsible for their actions within the simulation.
* Users must report any ethical concerns or violations.
